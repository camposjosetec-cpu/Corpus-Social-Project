About the League

Corpus Co‑Ed Sand League is a community‑first, adult social sports league. Expect real teams, set schedules, referees, standings, shirts, and a fun championship night — all centered around Corpus’s beach vibe.

We welcome complete beginners through casual athletes. Bring friends or sign up solo, we’ll place free agents onto teams.

Season Format

  • Teams: 8 teams • roster 6–8 players
  • Rules: 6×6 co‑ed, best‑of‑3 sets to 21 (cap 25)
  • Schedule: 5 regular‑season weeks + playoffs (Week 6)
  • Courts: 2 courts • 2 matches per night (4 total)
  • Gear: Volleyballs & equipment provided
  • Shirts: League shirts included for registered players

Pricing
